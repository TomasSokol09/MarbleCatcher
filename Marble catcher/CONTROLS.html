Marble catcher - Controls

Závěrečná práce

    Controls
  • Move left: A or ←
  • Move Right: D or →
    Rules
  • Collect as many marbles as you can
  • When your streak is higher than 5, you get bonus marbles
  • If you let 3 red marbles fall on the ground the game ends
  • If you collect a yellow marble, it counts as 3 marbles
  • If you collect a bomb (colored black) the game instantly ends
